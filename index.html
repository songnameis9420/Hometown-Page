Top three activities to do at Hanoi

Hoan Kiem lake is a perfect for you to walk around. The lake is surrounded by several species of trees, some of which are centuries old.

Great views across West Lake - and standard reasonably priced cafe fare.

Eat the best food in Vietnam

Despite looking simple outside, Pho has the fascinating complexity of textures and flavors.
